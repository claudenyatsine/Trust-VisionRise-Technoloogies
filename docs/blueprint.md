# **App Name**: GuardianSight Solutions

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Hero Section: A prominent, responsive hero section with key service highlights and a lead generation form for quick inquiries.
- Service Portfolio Display: A structured grid using shadcn/ui cards to showcase various CCTV installation services with brief descriptions.
- Lead Generation Form Submission: Backend handling for submitting lead generation forms, capturing potential customer information.
- About & Contact Pages: Dedicated pages for company information, values, and essential contact details.
- AI-Powered Service Explainer: A conversational tool that provides instant answers to common questions about CCTV systems and installation, leveraging company-specific data.
- Responsive Navigation: An intuitive, responsive navigation menu ensuring easy access to all sections of the website on any device.

## Style Guidelines:

- A deep, dark navy blue background (#191F2E) sets a foundation of trust and industrial professionalism, fostering a modern feel.
- A rich, vibrant gold (#E0A923) serves as the primary accent and interactive color, highlighting call-to-actions and important information.
- A warm red-orange accent color (#D95540) is used sparingly to draw attention to critical alerts or secondary interactive elements, providing a bold contrast to the gold.
- Primary font and button color: A dark blue (#032B5F) for high contrast and readability.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-inspired, industrial aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, high-trust readability across all content.
- Utilize clean, sharp, outline-style icons related to security, surveillance, and technology to maintain a modern and professional image.
- Adopt a structured, grid-based layout with generous spacing, enhancing readability and emphasizing key content such as the responsive hero and services grid.
- Implement subtle transition animations, such as gentle fades or element reveals on scroll, ensuring a premium feel without complex motion.
